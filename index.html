About Us

Our Mission

Kolypso Charity was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide life-saving support to children across Africa.

Our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive. We work directly with local communities to identify their most pressing needs and develop sustainable solutions that create lasting change.

Through partnerships with local organizations, governments, and international agencies, we maximize our impact and ensure that resources are used efficiently to benefit those who need them most.

Our Vision

We envision an Africa where every child has access to nutritious food, clean water, quality education, and healthcare.

Our vision extends beyond immediate relief to long-term transformation. We see communities that are self-sufficient, resilient, and empowered to shape their own futures.

By investing in children today, we're building the leaders of tomorrow who will continue the work of creating a more equitable and prosperous Africa for all.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of professionals and volunteers work tirelessly across 15 African countries to deliver aid.

Our team comprises experts in international development, public health, education, agriculture, and emergency response. Over 85% of our staff are local hires, ensuring cultural understanding and community trust.

We also work with thousands of volunteers who contribute their time, skills, and passion to further our mission. Their dedication is the backbone of our organization.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've provided assistance to over 2 million children and their families across Africa.

In the past year alone, we've distributed over 15 million meals, built 45 schools, provided clean water to 120 communities, and offered medical care to 75,000 children.

Our programs have helped reduce child mortality rates by up to 40% in the communities we serve, and school enrollment has increased by 60% in areas where we've implemented our education initiatives.
