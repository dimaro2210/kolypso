About Us

Our Mission

Kolypso Charity was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide life-saving support to children across Africa.

Our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive. We work directly with local communities to identify their most pressing needs and develop sustainable solutions that create lasting change.

Through partnerships with local organizations, governments, and international agencies, we maximize our impact and ensure that resources are used efficiently to benefit those who need them most.

Our Vision

We envision an Africa where every child has access to nutritious food, clean water, quality education, and healthcare.

Our vision extends beyond immediate relief to long-term transformation. We see communities that are self-sufficient, resilient, and empowered to shape their own futures.

By investing in children today, we're building the leaders of tomorrow who will continue the work of creating a more equitable and prosperous Africa for all.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of professionals and volunteers work tirelessly across 15 African countries to deliver aid.

Our team comprises experts in international development, public health, education, agriculture, and emergency response. Over 85% of our staff are local hires, ensuring cultural understanding and community trust.

We also work with thousands of volunteers who contribute their time, skills, and passion to further our mission. Their dedication is the backbone of our organization.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've provided assistance to over 2 million children and their families across Africa.

In the past year alone, we've distributed over 15 million meals, built 45 schools, provided clean water to 120 communities, and offered medical care to 75,000 children.

Our programs have helped reduce child mortality rates by up to 40% in the communities we serve, and school enrollment has increased by 60% in areas where we've implemented our education initiatives.

Discover Our Programs

Food Security

Our agricultural programs help communities grow nutritious food and become self-sufficient.

We implement sustainable farming techniques, provide quality seeds and tools, and train local farmers in modern agricultural practices. Our food security initiatives have helped reduce malnutrition rates by 45% in participating communities.

We also establish community gardens and school feeding programs to ensure children have access to nutritious meals daily.

Education for All

We build schools, train teachers, and provide scholarships to ensure every child has access to quality education.

Our education programs focus on building safe learning environments, providing educational materials, and training teachers in modern pedagogical methods. We've constructed over 200 classrooms and provided scholarships to more than 5,000 students.

We also implement digital literacy programs and establish computer labs to prepare children for the digital age.

Community Development

We work with local communities to develop sustainable solutions that address their unique challenges.

Our community development approach involves participatory planning where community members identify their priorities and co-create solutions. We've implemented over 300 community-led projects ranging from clean water access to healthcare facilities.

We also focus on empowering women and youth through skill development programs and microfinance initiatives that create economic opportunities.

Who We Help

Children in Conflict Zones

We provide emergency aid, psychological support, and education to children affected by war and conflict.

In conflict-affected areas, we establish safe spaces for children, provide trauma counseling, and ensure access to education even in the most challenging circumstances. Our mobile schools have reached over 50,000 children in conflict zones.

We also work to reunite separated families and provide legal support for children's rights protection.

Rural Communities

We support remote villages with access to clean water, healthcare, and educational resources.

Our rural development programs focus on integrated community development. We've drilled over 150 wells, established 80 health clinics, and built 120 schools in remote areas across 12 African countries.

We also implement renewable energy solutions like solar power to bring electricity to off-grid communities, improving quality of life and enabling economic activities after dark.

Children with Medical Needs

We provide medical treatments, surgeries, and ongoing care for children with health challenges.

Our medical programs provide life-saving treatments for conditions like malaria, malnutrition, and preventable diseases. We've performed over 5,000 surgeries and provided medical care to more than 100,000 children.

We also train local healthcare workers and establish community health programs to ensure sustainable healthcare solutions.

Our Impact

Nutrition

Over 1.2 million meals provided to malnourished children in the past year alone.

Our nutrition programs focus on both emergency feeding and long-term food security. We operate 45 feeding centers across East Africa and have reduced severe acute malnutrition by 60% in the communities we serve.

We also provide nutrition education to parents and caregivers, teaching them how to prepare balanced meals with locally available ingredients.

Education

75,000+ children now have access to quality education through our school programs.

Our education initiatives have built 120 schools, trained over 2,000 teachers, and provided scholarships to 15,000 students. School attendance has increased by 70% in communities where we work.

We also focus on girls' education, providing sanitary facilities and menstrual hygiene management to keep girls in school throughout their teenage years.

Clean Water

250+ wells built, providing clean water to over 500,000 people in rural communities.

Our water initiatives include drilling wells, installing rainwater harvesting systems, and building sanitation facilities. The time saved from fetching water has allowed children, especially girls, to attend school regularly.

We also establish water committees in each community to maintain the water points and ensure their sustainability for years to come.

Success Stories

Amina's Journey

From unable to read to top of her class - how education changed Amina's life forever.

Amina was 12 years old and had never attended school when we met her in a remote Kenyan village. Through our scholarship program, she enrolled in school and received extra tutoring. Despite starting later than her peers, Amina's determination saw her excel academically.

Today, Amina is at the top of her class and dreams of becoming a doctor. "Education has given me hope for a better future," she says. "I want to help other children in my village access the opportunities I've been given."

Samuel's Recovery

How life-saving surgery gave Samuel a second chance at a healthy childhood.

Samuel was born with a congenital heart defect that made even simple activities like walking difficult. His family couldn't afford the surgery he needed. Through our medical program, Samuel received life-saving surgery at a partner hospital.

Today, Samuel is an active 8-year-old who loves playing soccer with his friends. "I can run now without getting tired," he says with a bright smile. His recovery has inspired his community to prioritize children's healthcare.

Water for Wangechi Village

How a new well transformed daily life for an entire community in Kenya.

For generations, the women of Wangechi village walked 10 kilometers daily to fetch water from a contaminated river. Children often missed school to help with water collection, and waterborne diseases were common.

After we drilled a well in the center of the village, everything changed. Children's school attendance increased by 65%, and waterborne diseases decreased by 80%. The community now grows vegetables using the water, improving nutrition and creating a source of income.
